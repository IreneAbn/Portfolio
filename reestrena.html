Reestrena is a small second-hand women's clothes shop located in Madrid.

The redesign of its website focuses on cultivating customer's trust in second-hand online shopping.

UX solutions

I designed a clean Home Screen with two main buttons and a tab bar. Following the premise of the user's lack of clarity, the app will generate a random activity from the chosen theme but letting the users decide to carry on, generate another or choose one from the library.
